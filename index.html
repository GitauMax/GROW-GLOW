Our Services

Services That We Offer You

AGRONOMY TRAININGS

Our agronomists will walk the crop with you and advice on the best approaches for maximum yield and cost-effective production. We provide specialized trainings covering advanced crop production techniques. Our trainings include up-to-date information enabling farmers to make informed growing decisions.

CROP PROTECTION

We expertly diagnose crop diseases and scout for pests developing tailored spray programs that are aligned with the Insecticide Resistance Action Committee (IRAC) and Fungicide Resistance Action Committee (FRAC) guidelines, ensuring effective and sustainable use of chemical controls. Our IPM approach keeps your crops free from pests and diseases.

CROP NUTRITION

We identify specific nutritional deficits in your crops and formulate appropriate nutrition programs. Our expert guidance ensures your fertilization strategy is finely tuned to your soil’s unique needs, maximizing efficiency.

FARM START-UPS AND MARKET ANALYSIS

We assist you in starting your dream farm with a strong focus on market analysis and supply chain logistics. We provide an analysis into the most lucrative enterprise that meets current market needs This approach ensures your farm remains competitive and profitable.

FOOD SAFETY MANAGEMENT

We prioritize hygiene and food safety management in all our services, ensuring that your produce meets stringent safety standards required by both local and international markets. Our protocols help you implement effective control measures, from field to fork. This helps in building trust with consumers and retailers.

NUTRIENT STEWARDSHIP
(SOIL HEALTH)

We guide you in the correct management of fertilizers, focusing on achieving the best social, economic, and environmental outcomes. Our recommendations help you maintain soil fertility and structure.
